Pancake Ingredients

Ingredients You'll Need

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (or your preferred flavouring)

These ingredients will make around 6-8 fluffy pancakes!
