How To Prepare Pancakes

Step-by-Step Instructons

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla(or any flavour of your choice).
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined (a few lumps are okay).
  4. Heat a lightly buttered pan or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter per pancake and cook until bubbles form on top (about 2 minutes).
  6. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.
