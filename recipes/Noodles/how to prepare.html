How to Prepare Noodles

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add the noodles and seasoning sachet; stir gently.
  3. Allow to cook for about 2-3 minutes until the noodles soften.
  4. In a separate pan, heat vegetable oil and fry the sausage slices until golden brown.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the sausage and toss together.
  6. Mix in the chopped onions and green peppers for extra flavor.
  7. Serve hot and garnish with a few fresh leaves of parsley or basil.
