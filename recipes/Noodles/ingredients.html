Ingredients For Noodles

What You'll Need

  • 1 pack instant noodles
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 sausages (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion (chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (optional, diced)
  • A handful of leafy garnish (e.g., spinach or spring onions)
  • 1 seasoning sachet (comes with noodles)

These ingredients make a simple, flavorful noodle dish with sausage and greens.
